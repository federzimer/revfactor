### 2.3. Top 3 Problems / Frustrations

*Pre-filled examples from website (all valid):*
- "Pricing without strategy is just guessing"
- "Leaving money on the table during peak demand periods"
- "Gap nights and inconsistent occupancy eating into profits"
- "Reacting to competitors blindly"
- "Missing local events, seasonal shifts, and market changes"

**Top 3 based on Federico's actual client conversations:**

1. **ADR obsession over RevPAR** — Hosts fixate on nightly rate ("$1,000/night!") without tracking actual revenue generated. Federico: "I asked her how much she was selling. Silence." They're optimizing the wrong metric.

2. **Set-and-forget tool failure at scale** — Pricing tools work passably for 1–2 properties. When hosts scale to 5+, the tool runs but nobody's watching pacing, conversion rates, or competitive shifts. Revenue quietly leaks.

3. **Missing the new listing window** — Most hosts launch a new property at market rate and wait. Federico's approach: a specific market penetration strategy with a defined ramp-up period to build reviews fast and signal quality to the algorithm early. Most hosts never recover from a poor launch.

3. "I need to stay in control of my pricing" → Federico provides strategy and daily execution while keeping owners informed. Peace of mind, not loss of control.
