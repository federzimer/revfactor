Let me show you three things you can only do on the Airbnb app, but not on the website. If you scroll down to check-in instructions under your listing, you can create a detailed photo guide on how to check in into your property. You just have the check-in button enabled to get access to it. If you go to the amenities section on your listing, you'll be able to add the Wi-Fi speed of your property. For this, you'll have to be connected to the network, hit on test, and then your fast Wi-Fi speed will be showcased in your listing. If you started creating a listing draft and you won't be completing it, the only way to get rid of this annoying previous listing is by press and hold on it in the app and permanently deactivating it.
