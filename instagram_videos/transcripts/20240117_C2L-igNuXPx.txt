Having a mission statement is going to give you clarity on every decision that you make when you are just developing and growing your business. If you think about it, your mission statement is going to be your goal. It's going to be what you're aiming for. In the case of my business, my mission statement is to provide a five-star stay for my guest and peace of mind to my customers. My customers being my clients. So every time I'm making a decision, I'm writing a policy, I'm keeping that mission statement in mind. I'm making sure I'm still loyal to that original idea that my business said as a goal.
